MY HOBBY
Everyone has their own favourite thing to do in their leisure time that also known as hobby. Hobby is considered to be a regular activity that is done for enjoyment and participation in hobbies encourages acquiring substantial skills and knowledge in that area. I also has my own favourite hobbies which are listening to music, collecting trading card games, and playing video games.
Firstly, one of my hobbies is listening to music. I like to hear music from many genre to fill my past time. I also like to listen to comforting soundtrack to keep me focus when doing assignment and other works.
Secondly, I also like to collect trading card games as a precious collection of my own. I often collect any trading cards that has a specific market value due to its rarities or anything else that has taken my liking. My collection consists of different types of card games that I put in a well-sealed albums.
Finally, my last hobbies is playing video games. I enjoy playing different types of video games whether they are online or offline that came from many genres like RPG, MOBA, FPS or even gacha. Unfortunately, I only can play video games through my smartphone as my laptop is unable to support gaming feature.
